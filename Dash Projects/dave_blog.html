Essentials to Life

  • Love
  • Peace
  • Happiness

Amor

Love is friendship that has caught fire. It is quiet understanding, mutual confidence, sharing and forgiving. It is loyalty through good and bad times. It settles for less than perfection and makes allowances for human weaknesses.

Paz

Peace is a daily, a weekly, a monthly process, gradually changing opinions, slowly eroding old barriers, quietly building new structures. Peace cannot be kept by force; it can only be achieved by understanding. Darkness cannot drive out darkness; only light can do that.

Felicidad

There is only one way to happiness and that is to cease worrying about things which are beyond the power of our will.
